Sentiment Analysis of Call Recording

This page provides an analysis of the sentiment expressed in call recordings. The results are displayed in cards below, showcasing various sentiments detected.

Positive Sentiment

This indicates a favorable or optimistic tone. The text expresses feelings of happiness, satisfaction, approval, or support.

Neutral Sentiment

This indicates a lack of strong emotion. The text is often factual, objective, or simply informative without expressing any opinion or emotional tone.

Negative Sentiment

This indicates an unfavorable or pessimistic tone. The text conveys feelings of dissatisfaction, disappointment, anger, or criticism.
